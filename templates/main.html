About Us

NBM Hospital is under construction and will have 100 beds facility. In the near future, it will be expanded to Medical Institution to provide H. A. , Staff Nurse, Pharmacist etc. and ultimately to Medical College that will produce MBBS doctors. Every year we have planned to add 100 beds to our hospital making it 500 beds multi-specialty Medical College in five years. This will help to meet the increasing demand of qualified manpower of health sector of the nation.

The Hospital will be expanded outside the valley as NBM branch hospitals, each branch consisting of 15 beds. The priority for establishing such branch hospitals will be given to Himalayan and hilly districts /communities where no hospital exists. The mobile hospitals will also be launched in emergency cases or to provide the health services to remote areas
